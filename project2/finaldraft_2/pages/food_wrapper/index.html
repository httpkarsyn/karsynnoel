Think Before You Buy!

    The Consequences of Overconsumption

  • Earth will expire by 2050
  • By 2050, polluted air will kill 3 times as many people as it
    already has
  • Global oil reserves could be entirely depleted by 2050
  • If we continue to use our planets resources faster than
    they can be generated, we will eventually run out of
    resources, which would quite literally threaten humanitys
    existence

    Overconsumptions Impact on the Enviornment and Health

  • Thanks to the harmful chemicals that are released into the air during the oil extraction
    process, it now rains acid in the Niger Delta
  • Many residents of the delta suffer from breathing problems and chronic bronchitis,
    and a 2021 study found that cancer rates are much higher in the delta than in
    non-oil-producing parts of Nigeria
  • In Brazil, almost 20 percent of the entire Amazon has been deforested
  • Deforestation has also killed scores of animals who live in the Amazon
    and endangered the livelihoods of local indigenous people.

    How to Limit Your Own Overconsumption

  • There are plenty of practical steps individuals can take to
    reduce their consumption
  • Using reusable containers and products as opposed to
    disposable ones
  • Buying food in glass containers instead of plastic ones
  • Another way of fighting overconsumption is to reduce your meat
    and dairy consumption
  • Buy quality items that will last you a long time
  • Think before you buy
  • Stop impulsively buying

    Note to Self

  • Overconsumption is complex

    Blaming individuals isn't fair

    Yet, our current practices threaten
    ecosystems and human survival
    Without change, overconsumption
